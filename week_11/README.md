# Week 11
## Reinforcement Learning II

Lecture by [Kirill Khrylchenko](https://github.com/rita1302).

Seminar by [Artem Matveev](https://github.com/matfu-pixel)


Youtube:
* [Lecture](https://youtu.be/1-cnv2OOkuw)
* [Seminar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U-KBYvhjPbc)

In Week 11, we build a bridge between next-item prediction and reinforcement learning. We then discuss the REINFORCE algorithm in detail, including the all-actions and reward-to-go variants, REINFORCE with a baseline, and off-policy correction.

During the seminar, we discuss the following paper about RL application in YouTube recommendations: [Top-K Off-Policy Correction for a REINFORCE Recommender System](https://arxiv.org/abs/1812.02353). We also briefly mention [Off-Policy Learning in Two-Stage Recommender Systems](https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3366423.3380130) and [Off-Policy Actor-Critic for Recommender Systems](https://dl.acm.org/doi/pdf/10.1145/3523227.3546758).
