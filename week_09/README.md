# Week 9
## Ads ranking at Yandex

Lecture by [Margarita Mishustina](https://github.com/KhrylchenkoKirill)

Youtube:
* [Lecture](https://youtu.be/O8BrA5hT7bY)

In Week 9, we have a guest lecture by Margarita Mishustina, team/tech lead of the deep learning ads ranking models team at Yandex.

During the lecture, we:
1. Review the full ads ranking pipeline: index creation, retrieval, pre-ranking, ranking, re-ranking, and auction.
2. Provide an in-depth discussion of the ranking stage, with a focus on early fusion-based deep learning models.
