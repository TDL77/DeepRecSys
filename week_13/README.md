# Week 9
## Sber AI Lab RecSys Papers Review

Lecture by [Alexey Vasilev](https://github.com/AleXXL1986).

Youtube:
* [Lecture](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6OSjw4upu3g)

In the final week, we host a guest lecture by Alexey Vasilev, team lead of the recommender systems research team at Sber AI Lab. Alexey walks us through several papers from his team, covering both evaluation-focused work — including metrics, train-test splits, and sequential recommendation datasets — and algorithmic contributions, such as comparisons of SASRec and BERT4Rec and methods for improving cold-start recommendations by fusing content-based and ID-based embeddings.
