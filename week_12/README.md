# Week 12
## Generative Recommendations

Lecture by [Kirill Khrylchenko](https://github.com/rita1302).

Seminar by [Vladimir Baikalov](https://github.com/NonameUntitled)


Youtube:
* [Lecture](https://youtu.be/BbWTrvwaejg)
* [Seminar](https://youtu.be/CW0X_rY7wzs)

In Week 12, we discuss all things generative, including generative ranking (HSTU) and generative retrieval (TIGER), as well as recent efforts by Kuaishou to move towards one-stage recommender systems with OneRec. We also discuss conversational recommender systems and highlight YouTube's PLUM and Spotify's NEO models.

During the seminar, we discuss the seminal paper on semantic IDs and generative retrieval for recommendations: [Recommender Systems with Generative Retrieval](https://arxiv.org/abs/2305.05065).
